How to Use Aqua Daily Briefing

The Daily Briefing system combines your Outlook Data (Emails & Meetings) with your Daily Priorities into a single, easy-to-read report.

The Two-Part System

The system consists of two parts working together:

  1. The Web Dashboard: Where you manually type your tasks and project updates.
  2. The Outlook Agent: A small program on your PC that automatically grabs your emails and calendar.

Step 1: Manage Projects/Tasks (Web Dashboard)

Add/edit your projects/tasks/daily tasks!

  1. Go to the Admin Dashboard.
  2. Log in with your email and password.
  3. Daily Tasks: Add your top 3-5 priorities for the day.
  4. Projects: Update the status (e.g., "On Track", "Delayed") of your active projects.

Tip: You can do this from home or your phone! The data saves to the cloud instantly.

Step 2: Sync Outlook (The Agent)

Since the web cannot see your local Outlook, you must run the "Agent" to sync that data.

  1. Ensure Microsoft Outlook is open on your computer.
  2. Double-click G:\AquaDailyBriefing.exe.
  3. Wait a few seconds for the confirmation popup.

This uploads your Unread Emails (Last 24h) and Upcoming Meetings (Today-Friday) to the report.

Step 3: View Your Report

Once both steps are done, simply open the viewer link.

Usually: https://dailyaquabriefing.github.io/?daily=YOUR_ID

You will see your Web Tasks and your Outlook Data combined into one view.

Pro-Tip: Automate the Outlook Sync

Don't want to manually run the Agent every morning? Set it to run automatically!

Option A: Startup Folder (Runs once at login)

  1. Press Win + R on your keyboard.
  2. Type shell:startup and press Enter.
  3. Right-click inside the folder and select New > Shortcut.
  4. Browse to G:\AquaDailyBriefing.exe and click Finish.

Option B: Task Scheduler (Runs hourly)

This keeps your email list fresh all day.

  1. Open the Task Scheduler app.
  2. Click Create Basic Task....
  3. Name it "Briefing Sync".
  4. Trigger: Select "Daily" at "7:00 AM".
  5. Action: "Start a program" -> Browse to G:\AquaDailyBriefing.exe.
  6. Finish. Then right-click the task > Properties > Triggers > Edit.
  7. Check Repeat task every: 1 hour for a duration of 10 hours.

Note: If running automatically, you might want to open G:\dailybriefingconfig.txt and change Prompt=Yes to Prompt=No so it runs silently in the background.
